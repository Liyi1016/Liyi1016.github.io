  • This book is about my personal experience in Los Angeles alongside Reyner Banham's perspective of Los Angeles' architecture. The format was inspired by John Cage's music piece 4'33'', where the performers do not to play instruments during the entire duration of the piece. The break of instruments and the sounds of the environment that the listeners hear while it is per-formed become the content of the piece. I recorded 4'33'' of my experiences in various restaurant and public places I had been. The break of music is represented through the typography and book structure.
